Welcome To CCC

COMMUNITY Conference Center (CCC) is the only multi-purpose convention facility in the country, having 17 (seventeen) venues for holding small to large scale events, i.e, state functions, social events, seminars, conferences, product launches, annual general meetings, fairs, exhibitions, cultural programs, reality shows, etc. CCCI has been the venue for many renowned economic, social and cultural events. It has proudly hosted a number of international conferences and summits.
